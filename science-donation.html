Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to Science Donation, a WebView-based educational application available on Google Play. The platform is operated by a university-level instructor and provides academic content related to industrial chemical preparations (such as paints, coatings, detergents, and similar formulations) strictly for educational and scientific learning purposes only.

1. Educational & Safety Disclaimer

All content on Science Donation is provided for academic study and theoretical understanding. The platform does not promote or support commercial manufacturing, industrial production, or unsafe practical application of chemical formulations.

Users are solely responsible for how they use the information provided. Science Donation and its instructors are not responsible for misuse, unsafe practices, or real-world application outside an educational context.

2. Information We Collect

When users register or use the platform, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Login credentials (passwords are stored securely)
  • Subscription or enrollment status

We do not collect government IDs, health data, or sensitive personal information.

The application does not store or process credit card or financial information directly.

4. Permissions & Device Access

The application may request access to:

  • Camera & Microphone: For live lectures, recorded sessions, or interactive educational activities (if enabled by the instructor).
  • Storage: To cache or download educational materials.

Location data is NOT collected.

5. Assignments, Quizzes & Progress Tracking

The platform may collect and store educational activity data such as:

  • Video watch progress
  • Quiz and exam results
  • Assignment submissions
  • Course completion status

This data is used solely for educational evaluation and instructor feedback.

6. Third-Party Services

Science Donation does not sell or share user data with third-party advertisers. No external analytics or marketing SDKs are used.

Some educational content may include embedded videos (such as YouTube). These services may collect limited usage data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide educational services and manage subscriptions. Data may be deleted when accounts are inactive or upon request.

8. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request account or data deletion by contacting the platform administrator or instructor directly. Requests will be processed within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Age Requirement

Science Donation is intended for university students and adult learners. The app is not designed for children under the age of 16.

10. Security

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
