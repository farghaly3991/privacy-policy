Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Khaled Mersal educational app (package: com.farwep.khaled_mersal), developed and maintained as a private educational platform for students learning English. The application is designed to provide lessons, grammar explanations, vocabulary, reviews, and quizzes to help students improve their English skills.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information during account registration or when provided by the teacher:

  • Full name
  • Email address (if required)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password

This information is used solely to manage student accounts and track learning progress. No sensitive or financial information is collected.

2. Permissions Required

The application may request access to the following device features if needed for specific educational activities:

  • Microphone: For pronunciation practice or speaking exercises, as required by the teacher.
  • Storage: To cache educational content locally for offline access.

Location access is NOT requested.

3. No Third-Party Data Sharing

This application does not share, sell, or distribute user data to any third-party services. No integration with advertising networks, analytics tools, or external platforms is used. All data remains private to the teacher's system and the app's secure backend.

4. Use of Third-Party Content

The app may include embedded YouTube videos for educational purposes. These videos are streamed directly from YouTube's servers. YouTube may collect standard usage data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We do not control YouTube's data practices.

The app does not use any other third-party content or services.

5. Tracking Learning Progress

The app tracks the following learning-related data internally:

  • Lesson completion
  • Quiz scores
  • Vocabulary progress

This data is used exclusively by the teacher to monitor and improve student performance. It is not shared with any external system.

6. Data Retention

Student data is retained for the duration of the academic course or as long as the student's account is active. At the end of the course or upon request, data will be deleted.

7. Data Deletion Requests

Students or parents wishing to have their data removed may contact the teacher or developer directly via email at support@khaledmersalapp.com or through the contact information provided within the app. There is no in-app deletion feature; however, all requests will be processed promptly.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for students of all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it immediately. Teachers and parents are responsible for obtaining any necessary parental consent for students under 13.

9. Security

All data is stored securely on the app's backend server. Reasonable technical and administrative safeguards are in place to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of student data.
